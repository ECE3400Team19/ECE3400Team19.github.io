ECE 3400 Fall 2018

This semester we are in ECE 3400: Intelligent Physical Systems. Student teams are formed and throughout the semester work on various lab and milestone assignments, which ultimately will lead to the creation of a working, intelligent physical system: an autonomous robot that can traverse a maze, detect walls, detect other robots in its path, and have the ability to see and detect treasures of different shapes and sizes which are posted on various walls throughout the maze.
